Automatic PayPal Donation System.

The donation costs 10 EUR (incl. VAT).
After the donation you will receive 10 points + 2 bonus points (Tot. 12 points) in your account automatically.
Because you donate with PayPal / Creditcard you will receive +2 extra bonus points for free.

Here are the steps you need to make:
1. You need a valid creditcard or a PayPal account with a required balance [10 EUR].
2. Fill in your account number.
3. Click on the donate button.
4. Make a transaction on PayPal.
5. After the transaction 12 points will be automatically added to your account.
6. Go to Item shop and use your points
